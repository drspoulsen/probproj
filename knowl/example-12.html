Example 2.1.4.

Decide what the probability of each event would be better modeled by
(a) prob. of having a disease
(b) prob of heads twice in a row
(c) prob. of hand in poker
(d) prob. it rains
Solution.
(a) Bayesian
(b) Frequentist
(c) Frequentist
(d) Bayesian
Remark 2.1.5. Bonus!
The probability a sports team wins may be Bayesian, but the coin toss can be thought of as Frequentist!
